Bozeman, MT offers plenty of exciting opportunities to join in on mountain biking fun for competition/sport. Cycle the Continental Divide

The Great Divide is an off-pavement mountain biking route which cross-crosses the Continental Divide in southern Canada and the United States. This route is extremely remote, giving spectacular scenery and terrain, with almost the entire route being mostly dirt-road and mountain-pass riding. It has over 200,000 feet of elevation gain and is 2,100 miles of beauty. Come enjoy this thrilling, long-distance ride with fellow mountain bikers as we crown the victor of the 'Cycle the Continental Divide' once more in 2019!

  • Route: The Great Divide Mountain Biking Route (GDMBR)
